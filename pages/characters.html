Flint the Fire Ant

Flint is a short ant with an even shorter temper. His hobbies include making explosives, looting rapscallions, and throwing elaborate pranks on his crew mates. Flint loves being a problem-solver, as long as the solution can be “blow it up”. As a larvae, Flint was stolen from his home in a raid from a neighboring colony. They raised him as a working slave. When they realized he was more skilled at demolition than construction, they kicked him out. Flint by instinct made his way back to his birthplace, but his colony scent had irreversibly changed, so he was rejected. Ever since then, he’s never really fit in anywhere… until he met Captain Kate. She and her crew seem to appreciate his knack for destruction. He's gotten them out of more than a few sticky situations. Flint considers the crew to be his family… but if you ask him about it, he’ll probably blow a fuse.
